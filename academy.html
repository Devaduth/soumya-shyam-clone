Why choose us?

The first question you must ask yourself while choosing an academy is - Who is going to teach and how many real clients/brides has the artist done?


A lot of academies and artists have only teaching experience and very little real world experience of doing actual brides. Learning from them might give you a lot of theoretical knowledge but it may not get you ready to deal with real clients/brides with varied skin types, textures, face shapes, eye shapes, darkness, pigmentation, and requirements of real world.


We teach you makeup application that looks good not just in pictures but in real world in front of thousands of people and that lasts throughout the wedding. That is where our teaching differs from others.


Parul Garg, with her experience of doing thousands of real brides, teaches you the gist of what she has learned with those years of real world experience. She teaches her signature techniques of doing the base, eyes and everything that goes with it to make a perfect bride!

  • Signature Parul Garg Techniques
  • Hands on Practice on a daily basis
  • All Makeup Products for practice included in the course
  • Courses aimed at real world applications rather than textbook learning
